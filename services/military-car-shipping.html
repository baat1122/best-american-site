Tips & Tricks for Saving on Vehicle Shipping

Pro Tip for Military Car Shipping

Have your PCS orders ready, as some shipments may be partially reimbursable.

Flexible Pickup & Delivery

Choosing a five-day window for pickup and delivery can lower your shipping cost. Carriers can plan routes more easily with flexible dates, which means more options and better pricing for you.

Hub City Advantages

For your auto transport needs, choose a location near major highways. Spots close to major interstates attract more carriers, giving you more options and more competitive rates on your shipment.

Open Trailers

Open trailers cost less than enclosed trailers and work well for most standard vehicles. If your car doesn’t need extra protection from the elements, an open trailer is the smart, budget-friendly choice.

Military Car Shipping FAQs

Do you offer military discounts on car shipping? +
Yes, we offer special military discounts of 5-15% for active duty service members, veterans, and their families. Simply provide your military ID or proof of service when booking to receive the discount on any shipping route.
Can Neon Auto Transport ship my vehicle during a PCS move? +
Absolutely. We handle PCS (Permanent Change of Station) moves regularly and understand the tight timelines involved. We offer priority scheduling, flexible pickup dates, and can coordinate with your base transportation office as needed.
Will the military pay for my vehicle shipment? +
The military may reimburse one vehicle shipment during a PCS move if you meet certain eligibility requirements. Check with your transportation office about DITY (Do-It-Yourself) move reimbursement rates. We provide all documentation needed for claims.
Can you pick up or deliver vehicles on military bases? +
Yes, but base access requires advance coordination. The driver needs base clearance or we arrange pickup at the gate or a nearby off-base location. Contact us with your base name so we can plan the logistics accordingly.
How far in advance should I book military car shipping? +
For PCS moves, book at least 2-3 weeks before your report date. During peak PCS season (May-September), booking 4 weeks ahead is recommended. Expedited service is available if your move date is approaching quickly.

Factors that Affect Military Car Shipping Costs

Distance

The farther your car travels, the more you’ll pay. However, longer trips often cost less per mile than shorter ones.

Route

Shipping along major highways is cheaper and faster. Rural or off-the-beaten-path routes cost more due to limited carrier access.

Season

Summer and snowbird season drive prices up. Shipping during slower months, like fall or early spring, can save you money.

Vehicle Type

Larger vehicles like SUVs and trucks take up more space and use more fuel. Expect to pay more than you would for a standard sedan.

Traffic Patterns

Busy travel weeks, like Thanksgiving, can slow down delivery times. Booking during lighter traffic windows helps keep costs and delays down.

Carrier Availability

During peak season, carriers fill up fast. You may wait longer for pickup, so book early to lock in your preferred dates.

Open vs Enclosed

Open trailers are the most affordable option. Enclosed trailers offer extra protection but cost more — ideal for luxury or classic cars.

Population Density

Shipping to or from a busy city can cost more. Dense urban areas have more traffic and tighter access for large carriers.
