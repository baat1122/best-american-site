About Neon Auto Transport

A fully licensed, FMCSA-approved auto transport broker serving all 50 U.S. states since 2015.

Who We Are

Neon Auto Transport LLC is a fully licensed and insured auto transport broker headquartered at 2709 Neabsco Common Pl Suite 101, Woodbridge, VA 22191, United States. We have been providing nationwide car shipping services since 2015, with over 9 years of industry experience.

We are registered with the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) and hold active operating authority:

  • USDOT Number: 4355879
  • MC Number: 1703787
  • Legal Entity: Neon Auto Transport LLC
  • Business Type: Licensed Auto Transport Broker
  • Service Area: All 50 U.S. states + international shipping

As a broker, we connect customers with a vetted network of insured auto transport carriers. Every carrier in our network is verified for active cargo insurance, liability coverage, and safety compliance as required by the FMCSA. We have facilitated the safe shipment of over 150,000 vehicles.
