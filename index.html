      Delivery Timeline

      How long does it take to ship a car?

      Car delivery typically takes between 1 and 7 days, depending on distance and delivery route. The total shipping time is affected by five key factors: mileage, pickup and drop off locations, weather conditions, route congestion, and driver availability.

      🚛

      FMCSA Driving Rules

      According to the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA), solo drivers are permitted to travel 400 to 500 miles per day (about 8-10 hours of driving time). For expedited shipments, team drivers may cover 800 to 1,000 miles per day.

      ⏱️

      Standard Service Timeline

      Standard service uses solo drivers. To estimate your standard delivery time, divide your total route mileage by 500 miles per day.

      ⚡

      Expedited Service Timeline

      Expedited service utilizes dual team drivers. To estimate your expedited delivery time, divide your total route mileage by 1,000 miles per day.
