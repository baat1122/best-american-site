Privacy Policy

Learn how Neon Auto Transport collects, utilizes, retains, and guards your operational and personal data.

Introduction

Neon Auto Transport operates this website to provide auto transport brokerage services. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and trust of our visitors.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide, including name, email, phone number, pickup and delivery locations, and messages submitted through our forms. We also collect basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used to respond to quote requests, provide services, communicate about bookings and updates, offer customer support, and improve our website and services.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Information may only be shared if required by law, and only the minimum necessary will be disclosed.

4. SMS and Phone Communications

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive calls and SMS messages related to your inquiry and services. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP or by contacting us directly.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies to improve website performance and user experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data Security and Retention

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information. Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal requirements.

7. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information where legally permitted by contacting us directly at info@neonautotransport.com.
